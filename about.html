Our Mission

To make luxury accessible — delivering certified, handcrafted jewellery that speaks to every heart, every occasion, and every milestone. We craft not just jewellery, but heirlooms that outlast generations.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted jewellery destination — known for transparency, artistry, and an unwavering commitment to customer delight. We envision a world where every woman wears jewellery that tells her story.

Our Journey

A story of passion, growth, and golden milestones

2026 · January
The Beginning

Unique Creation Jewellers opens its first boutique near Shreyas Cinema, Ghatkopar, Mumbai — with a dream and a handful of handcrafted pieces.

2026 · April
First 500 Customers

Within three months, word-of-mouth brings us our first 500 happy families — a testament to quality and personal service that sets us apart.

2026 · August
Diamond Collection Launch

We launch our signature Diamond Jewellery line, offering certified solitaires and diamond studded pieces at transparent, honest prices.

2025 · February
Bridal Collection

Our exclusive Bridal Jewellery Collection is unveiled — earning recognition as one of Mumbai's finest destinations for wedding jewellery.

2025 · October
Online Launch — Nano Jewels

Nano Jewels goes digital — bringing our handcrafted luxury to customers across India, with free shipping and 7-day easy returns.

2026 · Present
5,000+ Happy Families

Today we serve over 5,000 happy customers with 1,200+ unique designs. The journey continues — one jewel, one story at a time.
